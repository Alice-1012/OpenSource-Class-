 This is a Python Sales Management Program Information
====================================================
1. Write a Python program processing sales orders
2. Contents in sales order : Date/Time of Sales, Cashier ID, Sales total, Card/Cash, Item name, Unit Price, Quantities, Sales Amount
3. Read all the sales orders from a file.
4. Generate a sales report(e.g. total, min, max, average revenue of daliy, day of the week, per cashier, per item, card/cash)
5. You can design your own sales report format.
6. # of items >= 10, # of cashiers >= 3, # of sales orders >= 100
7. Submit your report including Jupyter notebook, data, and documentation.
